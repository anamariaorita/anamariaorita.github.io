PhD Topic

Controllability of PDEs

I am a PhD student at Université Clermont Auvergne, based at the Blaise Pascal Mathematics Laboratory. My research focuses on the numerical analysis and controllability of second-order partial differential equations, including wave and Euler–Bernoulli beam equations. I am particularly interested in high-order finite difference schemes and the spectral analysis of discretized operators.
