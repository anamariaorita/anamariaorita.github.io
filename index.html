PhD Topic

Controllability of PDEs

I am a PhD student at Université Clermont Auvergne (LMBP), focusing on numerical analysis and controllability of second-order PDEs, including wave and Euler–Bernoulli beam equations. My research emphasizes high-order finite difference schemes and spectral analysis of discretized operators.
