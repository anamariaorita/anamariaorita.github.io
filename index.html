Introduction

About Me

I am a PhD student at Université Clermont Auvergne (LMBP). My research focuses on numerical analysis and controllability of second-order partial differential equations, including wave and Euler–Bernoulli beam equations. I am particularly interested in high-order finite difference schemes and spectral analysis of discretized operators.

Research Interests

  • Controllability of partial differential equations (PDEs)

  • Spectral analysis of discretized operators
  • Stability and convergence of numerical schemes
